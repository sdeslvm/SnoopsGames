Objective

Lead your rapper to the center of the arena by throwing fiery bushes into boxes and outmaneuvering your opponent!

How to Play

The rules are simple but packed with heat. Here's how it goes down:

  • Placing Rappers: At the start of the match, both players position their rappers in one of the boxes in the first row.
  • Throwing Fiery Bushes: The rapper selects a box and throws a fiery bush into it:
    • If the box is empty, they move forward and throw again.
    • If another rapper (ally or enemy) is inside, the attacker returns to the start.
  • Winning the Game: The first rapper to reach the center of the arena wins the round!

Who will reach the center and claim the crown of the battle? Step up and show your skills in "Snoops Game"!
